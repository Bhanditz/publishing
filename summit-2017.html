W3C Publishing Summit 2017

How Web Technologies are Shaping Publishing Today, Tomorrow, and Beyond

November 9-10, 2017
San Francisco
CA, USA

The Open Web Platform and its technologies have become indispensable to virtually all aspects of publishing. They're essential to how content is created, developed, enhanced, discovered, disseminated, and consumed. The new Publishing@W3C initiative is dedicated to advancing Web technologies to make them ever more useful for the creation and distribution of publications, online and offline.

The inaugural W3C Publishing Summit will show how publishers of all types are using today's Web technologies, including EPUB, to make their publications more effective and their workflows more efficient. You'll learn about things you should be doing now to improve your products and processes; developments coming soon that you need to be aware of; and what is coming next in web publications, EPUB, and the overall Web Platform that can support your workflows.

Come to the W3C Publishing Summit to join this critical conversation as publishing industry leaders cover issues and innovations you need to know about to navigate the rocky shoals of digital transformation --including real-world projects and techniques that you can use today.

A full program announcement is coming soon - but don’t wait: register now to secure your seat at what is expected to be a sell-out event.

The W3C Publishing Summit will be co-located with W3C’s main annual technical meeting, TPAC. , so attendees will have the opportunity to network with Web developers and technical experts, including access to a solutions showcase demo area.
